## 1. KONSEP BRAND

**Niche:** Fashion Old Money — kualitas di atas segalanya
**Target Market:** Pria/wanita 25-45 tahun, kelas atas, appreciate craftsmanship
**Positioning:** "Quiet luxury" — bukan brand yang teriak, tapi yang diam-diam mahal

**Ciri khas Old Money:**
- Kualitas > Kuantitas
- Warna netral (cream, navy, coklat tua, hijau botol)
- Material premium (kashmir, sutra, linen, kulit)
- Potongan klasik yang tak lekang waktu
- Branding minimalis, gak ada logo besar
- "Stealth wealth" — orang tahu mahal, tapi gak norak

## 3. TIPOGRAFI (dari UI UX Pro Max)

**Rekomendasi: 3 opsi font pairing**

### Opsi A: Luxury Serif ⭐ (RECOMMENDED)
- **Heading:** Cormorant (serif, elegan, refined)
- **Body:** Montserrat (sans-serif, geometric, precise)
- **Mood:** luxury, high-end, fashion, elegant, refined
- **Best for:** Fashion brands, luxury e-commerce, jewelry
- **Google Fonts:** `Cormorant:wght@400;500;600;700` + `Montserrat:wght@300;400;500;600;700`

### Opsi B: Luxury Minimalist
- **Heading:** Bodoni Moda (serif, high contrast)
- **Body:** Jost (sans-serif, geometric)
- **Mood:** luxury, minimalist, sophisticated
- **Best for:** Luxury minimalist brands, high-end fashion

### Opsi C: Classic Elegant
- **Heading:** Playfair Display (serif, timeless)
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif, clean)
- **Mood:** elegant, sophisticated, editorial
- **Best for:** Luxury brands, editorial, magazines

**Saran gw: Opsi A (Cormorant + Montserrat)**
- Cormorant punya **high contrast** yang sangat "old money"
- Montserrat bersih dan modern untuk body text
- Sudah terbukti dipakai brand luxury

## 5. UX GUIDELINES (dari UI UX Pro Max)

### Image Optimization (Severity: HIGH)
- ✅ Gunakan WebP format
- ✅ Responsive images (`srcset`)
- ❌ Jangan pakai 4000px untuk display 400px

### Navigation (Severity: MEDIUM)
- ✅ Active state jelas (color/underline)
- ❌ Semua link jangan sama style-nya

### Old Money UX Principles:
- **Less is more** — gak perlu banyak animasi
- **Typography carries personality** — bukan dekorasi
- **White space = luxury feel** — jangan takut space kosong
- **Product photos = hero** — foto besar, berkualitas tinggi
- **Micro-interactions halus** — hover lembut, transisi smooth

## 7. TONE OF VOICE

**Brand berbicara dengan gaya:**
- **Understated** → Gak perlu teriak, kualitas bicara sendiri
- **Confident** → Percaya diri tanpa sombong
- **Timeless** → Bukan trend, tapi klasik abadi
- **Refined** → Halus, sophisticated, berkelas

**Contoh copywriting:**

✅ Yang cocok:
- "Crafted for those who know the difference"
- "Quality speaks quietly"
- "Not for everyone. And that's the point."
- "Investment pieces, not impulse buys"

❌ Yang tidak cocok:
- "BEST DEAL!! DISKON 50%!!!" (norak)
- "Premium quality at affordable price" (kontradiksi)
- "Trend terbaru 2026!" (old money gak ikut trend)
- "Follow us for daily updates!" (too casual)
